Core Strategy Playbook

Guidelines for executing the 4 Visits + 4 Trainings package.

The Core Program Rule

Every core school is assigned an annual support plan consisting of 4 on-site support visits (V1–V4) and 4 interactive training workshops (T1–T4).

Operational Phase Guide

Phase 1: Baseline Assessment & V1

Complete baseline SSA assessment to isolate the two weakest focus areas. Schedule Visit 1 focusing on primary bottlenecks.

Phase 2: Training Blocks (T1 & T2)

Assemble school teams for target development workshops. Facilitate group exercises for classroom and leadership coaching.

Phase 3: Partner Collaboration (V2 & V3)

Assign specialized interventions to verified partners to leverage specific expertise in Reading Fluency or EdTech setup.

Phase 4: Impact Assessment & Graduation

Execute follow-up SSA. Verify evidence to clear accountability. Advance qualifying core schools to Champion status.
