5. Automated Notification Workflow

1 Pre-Course Reminder

7, 3, and 1 day before the course start date — to the employee.

2 In-Progress Check-In

Daily, while the course is under way — to the employee.

3 Mark Complete Prompt

Once the end date passes, until marked complete — to the employee.

4 Overdue Escalation

7, 14, and 30 days overdue and still not marked complete — to the employee.

5 Supervisor + HR Escalation

From 14 days overdue onward — to the supervisor and HR too.

Reminders are computed and sent by a daily scheduled job (send_pd_reminders). Every reminder is deduplicated, so no one is notified twice for the same milestone on the same day.
