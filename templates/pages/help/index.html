{# ── Role guides ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

CCEO — Field Execution

  • Plan your school visits in Planning — select schools, pick a day; the live route preview scores feasibility, travel load and cost per school before you confirm.
  • Meet the CD's daily visit target; below-target days require a reason.
  • Execute from My Plan: complete visits, upload evidence, enter the Activity SF ID.
  • Confirm your weekly fund request so it reaches your Program Lead for approval.
  • Watch To-Do — it derives live from your actual workflow state and clears itself when you act.

Program Lead — Team Command

  • Your dashboard is scoped to your supervised CCEOs only — targets, backlog, SSA risk, route quality.
  • Approve/return CCEO weekly fund requests from the dashboard queue; your own requests route to the CD.
  • Use PL Analytics for SSA-by-intervention, district/cluster performance and the school risk list.
  • Review completed activities in the PL queue before they proceed to IA verification.

Country Director — National Oversight

  • The CD dashboard surfaces what needs you today: escalated fund requests, the monthly budget stage, PL risk.
  • CD Analytics is the national intelligence cockpit — country-wide, drillable, oversight-only.
  • Own the Country Monthly Budget: review, add the admin plan, submit to the RVP.
  • Maintain Cost Settings and district classifications — scheduling depends on them.

IA · Accountant · RVP · HR

  • IA verifies completed work in the Verification Queue — evidence, duplicates, SSA uploads.
  • Accountant runs disbursement and accountability from the Disbursement Dashboard; NetSuite codes close the loop.
  • RVP approves or returns the country monthly budget from their dashboard.
  • HR manages leave approvals, coverage clashes and public holidays from the HR dashboard.
